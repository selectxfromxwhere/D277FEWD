Kalispell is one of the most breathtaking areas of Montana. The city began as a small outpost for railroad workers and was officially incorporated in 1892. Located in Flathead County, it sits at the northwestern part of the state in the Flathead Valley, near Glacier National Park. Kalispell is considered an urban micropolitan statistical area, having a population of just over 28,450 people.
