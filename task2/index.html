Welcome To Montana

Montana is the 41st state of the Union, known as Big Sky Country and the Treasure State.

Visit The Last Best Place

The Treasure State

Montana is one of the greatest options for outdoor activities, providing visitors and residents with outstanding natural beauty and a low population density.

People

Montana has a population of 1,084,225 and land area of 147,040 square miles making it one of the least populated states in the country.

Economy

Montana has an average cost of living, a median household income of $60,500 a low unemployment rate of 4.4%, and no sales tax. Most large employers are locally owned and operated.

Climate

On the West side of the Continental Divide, Montana is a dry mountain state. On the East, its a combination of high plains and scattered forested regions.

Discover Montana

Come visit! Helena - the historic capital city, Kalispell - the home of Flathead Lake, or Bozeman - the rapidly-growing tech hub.
